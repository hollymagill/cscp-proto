Pupils meeting expected standard in reading, writing and maths

The expected standard is a scaled score of 100 or more in reading and maths tests. A teacher also has to assess you as 'working at the expected standard' or better in writing.

Pupils meeting higher standard in reading, writing and maths

The higher standard is a scaled score of 110 or more in reading and maths tests. A teacher also has to assess you as 'working at a greater depth within the expected standard' in writing.
