Meeting standards in reading, writing and maths

2018 and 2019 scores are not directly comparable with 2017 scores.

How do pupils achieve the expected standard?

Pupils are meeting the expected standard if:

  • they achieve a scaled score of 100 or more in their reading and maths tests
  • and their teacher assesses them as 'working at the expected standard' or better in writing

How do pupils achieve the higher standard?

Pupils are achieving the higher standard if:
